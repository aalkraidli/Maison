Our Story

Void
Label

Philosophy

Less.
Better.
Always.

We started VLD in 2018 with one question: why does every brand make so much stuff? Two collections a year. Eight to twelve pieces each. Nothing more.

Every item is designed to still be in your wardrobe in ten years. We work with three factories — all family-owned, all in Europe, all paid fairly.

No sales. No markdowns. No overproduction. When it's gone, it's gone.
